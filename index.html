🌐 About Tamagosol
🦎
What is Tamagosol? A crypto-themed virtual pet game. Hatch a creature, raise it with care, and evolve it into legendary forms!
🪙
What is $SOGO? $SOGO is a fun, fictional in-game currency. It has zero real-world value and is not a financial product.
⛏
What is Hash Power? Hash Power represents your pet's mining ability. Higher Hash = more $SOGO earned when staking!
