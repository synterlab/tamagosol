🎮 How to Play
🥚
Hatch Your PetTap the egg to hatch a random species — Solbit, Aquabit, or Blazebit!
📊
Keep Stats UpHunger, Energy, Mood & Hash Power decay over time. Use Feed, Play, Stake & Clean to keep them high.
💎
Stake to EarnTap Stake to mine $SOGO passively. Higher Hash Power = more $SOGO per second.
🛒
Visit the ShopSpend $SOGO on powerful items: Revival Kits, Hash Chips and more!
✨
Evolve!Care well and your pet evolves: Baby → Teen → Adult → Diamond → Whale. Neglect it = 💀.
